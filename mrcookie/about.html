We are the Fuscos!

Pai

Pai is the steady foundation of our family and the heart behind the dream of Mr. Cookie becoming a reality. Originally from Brazil, he brings a lot of love, laughter, and flavor into everything we do. He works incredibly hard—behind the scenes and beyond—to support every part of our project, from planning and logistics to cheering us on through every sale. Pai believes deeply in teaching by example, and his dedication shows our kids the value of commitment and perseverance. Without him, Mr. Cookie wouldn't be more than a sweet idea—we're so grateful he's our biggest helper and believer.

Mom: Kane

Hi friends! I'm Daniela, the proud mom behind Mr. Cookie. Baking has always been one of my greatest joys, especially when I get to share it with my family. I’ve loved seeing how my children have inherited this passion and how we’ve grown together through this little business. I usually take the lead in baking (and yes, I happily work for kisses!), but I’m really just part of a beautiful team. More than anything, I want my kids to know that with love, effort, and teamwork, we can build something truly special.

Keto

Keto is our amazing swimmer—fast, focused, and always up for a challenge. Outside the water, he’s just as dedicated in the kitchen, where he helps measure, mix, and of course, taste-test our cookies. He has a great eye for details and often spots ways we can improve our process. Keto loves working side by side with his siblings and brings a lot of fun and energy to our team. He’s proof that discipline and sweetness can go hand in hand!

Dodo

Dodo is a bright and creative soul who sees the world in such a unique way. Being neurodiverse is one of his superpowers—it helps him notice things others might miss. He’s deeply involved in packing the cookies and always makes sure everything looks just right. Dodo brings joy and warmth to our kitchen, and his smile lights up every market we attend. We love how he reminds us that everyone has something important to contribute.

Totti

Totti is our little Ju Jitsu champ—strong, focused, and full of determination. He approaches cookie-making with the same passion he brings to the mat. Totti is always ready to help, whether it’s rolling dough or carefully placing cookies into their packages. He’s also one of our top salespeople and never shies away from sharing our treats with others. His courage and dedication inspire us every day.

Cece

Cece is the heart of the Fusco crew—the only girl, but definitely a strong and shining one! She’s curious, kind, and loves experimenting with new cookie ideas and flavors. Cece takes pride in helping with the packaging and always adds her special touch to make everything feel extra lovely. She has a natural sense of style and often suggests how we can present things better. Cece reminds us that sweetness comes in many forms—kindness, creativity, and a sprinkle of sparkle.
